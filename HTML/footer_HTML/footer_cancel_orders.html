Cancellation Policy

Thank you for choosing to shop with us. We value your patronage and strive to provide you with the best shopping experience possible. We understand that circumstances may arise where you might consider canceling your order. However, we would like to outline our cancellation policy to help you make an informed decision and ensure a smooth shopping process for all our customers.

1. Order Placement and Confirmation:

When you place an order with us, you will receive an order confirmation via email or other communication channels. This confirmation indicates that your order has been successfully placed in our system. It is important to review your order details carefully at this stage to avoid any discrepancies.

2. Cancellation Period:

We offer a limited cancellation period during which you can request to cancel your order. This period begins immediately after you receive the order confirmation and ends after one week. We encourage you to act promptly if you wish to cancel your order within this timeframe.

3. Cancellation Process:

To cancel your order, please contact our customer service team through the provided channels 054-180-9090. You will need to provide your order number and any relevant information to assist us in locating your order quickly. Our customer service representatives will guide you through the cancellation process and address any concerns you may have.

4. Cancellation Fees:

In order to cover the costs associated with processing orders, we may impose a cancellation fee for canceled orders. This fee is determined based on various factors, such as the order value, processing stage, and any restocking or administrative costs incurred. The cancellation fee will be deducted from the refund amount, if applicable.

5. Refund Process:

If your cancellation request is approved and meets the specified criteria, we will initiate the refund process. Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original transaction. Please note that it may take a few business days for the refund to be reflected in your account.

6. Exceptions to Cancellation:

Certain circumstances may restrict the cancellation of an order, such as:

  • Customized or personalized products that have already entered production.
  • Perishable goods or items with limited shelf life.
  • Digital products that have been downloaded or accessed.
  • Services that have been rendered or initiated.

7. Order Modifications:

If you need to make any changes to your order, such as updating the shipping address or modifying the product variant, please contact our customer service team within the cancellation period. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that order modifications are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

8. Considerations before Canceling:

Before initiating a cancellation, we encourage you to consider the following points:

  • Availability: The item you ordered may be in high demand, and canceling your order might result in losing the opportunity to purchase it again.
  • Price Changes: If there have been any price changes since your order was placed, canceling and reordering may result in a higher price.
  • Limited Stock: Some items may have limited availability, and canceling your order may make it difficult to secure the same product later.
  • Time Constraints: Canceling an order may cause delays in receiving the desired product, especially if it is a pre-order or limited edition item.

9. Contacting Customer Support:

If you have any questions, concerns, or need assistance regarding the cancellation of your order, our customer support team is available to help you. Please reach out to us through the provided communication channels, and we will be happy to assist you promptly.

We hope this policy provides you with a clear understanding of our cancellation procedures. We strive to offer a seamless shopping experience, and we appreciate your cooperation and understanding in adhering to this policy. If you have any further inquiries or require clarification, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Thank you for your support.
