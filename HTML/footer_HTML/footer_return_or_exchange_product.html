Return or Exchange of a Product

Dear Customer,

We have received your request for a return or exchange of a product purchased from our store. We understand that situations may arise where you need to return or exchange a product, and we are committed to assisting you in the process. Before proceeding with your request, we would like to provide you with important information regarding our return and exchange policy:

1. Eligibility for Return or Exchange:

In order to be eligible for a return or exchange, please ensure the following conditions are met:

  • The request for return or exchange is made within 14 days from the date of purchase.
  • The product is in its original condition, unused, and undamaged.
  • All original tags, labels, packaging, and accessories are intact and included.
  • The return or exchange is requested for a valid reason, such as manufacturing defects, incorrect size or color, or damaged during shipping.

2. Return or Exchange Process:

To initiate the return or exchange process, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team via our reception 054-180-9090 and provide your order number, the name of the product you wish to return or exchange, and the reason for the return or exchange.
  2. Our customer support team will guide you through the next steps, which may include providing additional information, photographs, or supporting documents.
  3. Once your request is reviewed and approved, you will receive further instructions on how to proceed with the return or exchange.
  4. Please securely pack the product in its original packaging, including all accessories and tags, and ship it back to us using a reliable shipping service. Ensure that you obtain proof of shipment.
  5. Upon receiving the returned product, our team will inspect it to verify its condition and eligibility for return or exchange.
  6. If the return or exchange is approved, we will process the refund or initiate the exchange as per your preference. Please note that refunds may take 7-10 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

3. Exclusions:

Please note that certain items may be excluded from our return or exchange policy for hygiene reasons, such as personal care products or intimate apparel. Additionally, custom-made or personalized items may not be eligible for return or exchange unless there is a manufacturing defect or error on our part.

If you have any questions, concerns, or need further assistance regarding the return or exchange process, please do not hesitate to contact our customer support team. We are here to ensure a smooth and satisfactory resolution for you.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
