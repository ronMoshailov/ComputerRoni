Request to Cancel or Change an Order

Dear Customer ,

We have received your request to cancel or change your order with us. We understand that circumstances may arise that necessitate modifications or cancellations, and we are here to assist you. However, before proceeding with your request, we would like to provide you with some important information to consider:

1. Order Status:

Please be aware that once an order is placed, it enters our system for processing and fulfillment. Therefore, the status of your order may vary depending on the time of your request. If your order has already been processed, shipped, or is in transit, cancellation or modification may not be possible.

2. Eligible Orders:

Not all orders may be eligible for cancellation or modification. Orders that are custom-made, personalized, or already in the final stages of fulfillment may not be eligible for changes. Additionally, orders placed during peak periods or sales events may have limited cancellation or modification options due to high order volumes and fast processing times.

3. Cancellation Policy:

If you wish to cancel your order, please review our cancellation policy:

  • Cancellation Timeframe: Requests for order cancellation must be submitted within one week of placing the order. We are unable to cancel orders beyond this specified timeframe as they may have already entered the processing or shipping stages.
  • Procedure: To request an order cancellation, please contact our customer support team immediately via our reception 054-180-9090. Provide your order number and a detailed explanation of your request. Our team will review your request and respond to you within 8:00 - 17:00.
  • Refund Process: If your cancellation request is approved, we will initiate the refund process. Please note that it may take 3-5 business days for the refunded amount to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

4. Modification Policy:

If you wish to make changes to your order, please review our modification policy:

  • Modification Timeframe: Requests for order modifications must be submitted within one week of placing the order. We cannot guarantee that modifications can be made after this specified timeframe as the order may have already entered the processing or shipping stages.
  • Eligible Modifications: Order modifications may include changes to the shipping address, product variant, or quantity. Please note that not all modifications can be accommodated due to inventory availability or logistical constraints.
  • Procedure: To request an order modification, please contact our customer support team immediately via our reception 054-180-9090. Provide your order number and clearly specify the changes you would like to make. Our team will review your request and respond to you within 8:00 - 17:00.

If you have any questions or need further clarification regarding the cancellation or modification process, please do not hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. We are here to assist you and provide the best possible solution based on your specific circumstances.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
